<p><p>Refrigerated Display Cases (RDC) glasses are essential for showcasing perishable products while maintaining temperature control. In contrast, double glass cases consist of two panes with an air gap, enhancing insulation and energy efficiency, making them ideal for long-term storage of sensitive items. Both types cater to diverse commercial needs in retail environments.</p></p>
