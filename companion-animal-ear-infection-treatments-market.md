<p><p>The companion animal ear infection treatments market is segmented based on the type of ear infections: Otitis Externa, Otitis Media, and Otitis Interna. Otitis Externa involves inflammation of the outer ear canal and is commonly treated with topical medications and cleansers. Otitis Media affects the middle ear, requiring systemic antibiotics and anti-inflammatory treatments. Otitis Interna, an infection of the inner ear, necessitates more aggressive treatments, often involving a combination of medications to address severe symptoms and prevent complications.</p></p>
