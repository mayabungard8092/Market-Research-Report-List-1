<p><strong>The Polishing Resin for Ultrapure Water Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>"Cation Exchange Resin"</li><li>"Anion Exchange Resin"</li><li>"Mixed Bed Resin"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ultrapure water market features three main types of polishing resins: </p><p>1. **Cation Exchange Resin**: Used to remove positively charged ions, enhancing water purity by capturing contaminants like calcium and magnesium. </p><p>2. **Anion Exchange Resin**: Focuses on eliminating negatively charged ions, targeting impurities such as sulfate and chloride. </p><p>3. **Mixed Bed Resin**: Combines both cation and anion exchange resins in a single vessel, providing comprehensive purification for high-grade water applications. Each type ensures the production of ultrapure water for sensitive industrial processes.</p></p>
