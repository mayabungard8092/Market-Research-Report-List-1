<p><p>The orthopedic cartilage repair market encompasses various surgical procedures targeting different joints and ailments. ACL (Anterior Cruciate Ligament) and PCL (Posterior Cruciate Ligament) repairs focus on stabilizing knee injuries, while meniscal repair addresses cartilage tears in the knee. Rotator cuff repairs target shoulder injuries, and shoulder labrum procedures restore stability. Hip arthroscopy involves minimally invasive techniques for hip joint issues, while biceps tenodesis relieves pain and restores function in the shoulder by reattaching the biceps tendon. Together, these treatments enhance joint function and patient quality of life.</p></p>
