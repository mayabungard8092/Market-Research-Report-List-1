<p><ul><li>Coil Tattoo Equipment</li><li>Rotary Tattoo Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tattoo equipment market comprises primarily two types: coil tattoo equipment and rotary tattoo equipment. Coil tattoo machines utilize electromagnetic coils to drive the needles, offering precision and versatility for detailed work, making them popular among traditional tattoo artists. In contrast, rotary tattoo machines operate using a motorized mechanism, allowing for smoother and quieter operation, which is favored for line work and shading. Both types cater to artists' preferences and styles, contributing to the diverse landscape of the tattoo industry.</p></p>
