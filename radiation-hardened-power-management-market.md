<p><p>The radiation-hardened power management market encompasses several key types of solutions designed to ensure reliability in space and other radiation-sensitive environments. Linear voltage regulators, including low-dropout regulators, provide stable output with minimal voltage loss. Point of load converters enable efficient power distribution by converting higher voltages to lower ones at the load site. PWM controllers regulate voltage based on pulse-width modulated signals, while gate drivers control power switches in various applications. Additional components and technologies also contribute to this specialized market segment.</p></p>
