<p><p>The nasal cannula market is categorized into low-flow and high-flow nasal cannulas. Low-flow nasal cannulas deliver oxygen at lower rates, typically up to 6 liters per minute, allowing patients to receive supplemental oxygen while maintaining some breathing room for ambient air. High-flow nasal cannulas provide a higher concentration of oxygen, often exceeding 6 liters per minute, and are designed to meet higher respiratory demands, making them suitable for critical care settings or patients with severe respiratory conditions.</p></p>
