<p><ul><li>Full Solar Powered Train</li><li>Non-full Solar Powered Train</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar-powered train technology market is divided into two main types: full solar-powered trains and non-full solar-powered trains. Full solar-powered trains rely entirely on solar energy, utilizing photovoltaic panels to generate the necessary power for operations, making them eco-friendly and sustainable. In contrast, non-full solar-powered trains incorporate solar energy as a supplementary source, using a combination of traditional fuels and solar power, thereby reducing emissions while still depending on conventional energy sources for a portion of their energy needs.</p></p>
