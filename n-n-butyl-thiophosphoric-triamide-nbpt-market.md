<p><p>N- (n-butyl) Thiophosphoric Triamide (NBPT) is a urease inhibitor widely used in agriculture to enhance nitrogen use efficiency in fertilizers, particularly urea. This chemical compound works by inhibiting the enzymatic conversion of urea into ammonia, thereby reducing nitrogen loss through volatilization. As a result, NBPT helps improve crop yields while minimizing environmental impact, making it a vital component in sustainable agriculture practices.</p><p>The market for NBPT is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of the need for efficient fertilizer management and the rising adoption of precision agriculture techniques.
