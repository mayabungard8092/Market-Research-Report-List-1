<p><ul><li>Piezoelectric Sensor</li><li>Acoustic Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The knock sensors market comprises primarily two types: piezoelectric sensors and acoustic sensors. Piezoelectric sensors detect vibrations and pressure changes, converting them into electrical signals, making them ideal for precise knock detection in engines. Acoustic sensors, on the other hand, analyze sound waves generated by engine operations, identifying knock events through sound frequency analysis. Both sensor types enhance engine performance and efficiency by enabling real-time monitoring, ultimately contributing to better fuel economy and reduced emissions in automotive applications.</p></p>
