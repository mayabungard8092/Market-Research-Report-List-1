<p><p>The automotive telematics insurance market consists of four main types: </p><p>1. **Hardwired** systems involve permanently installed devices that access the vehicle's data, offering comprehensive monitoring. </p><p>2. **On-Board Devices** are plug-and-play units that can be easily attached to the vehicle's diagnostic port. </p><p>3. **Smartphone** solutions utilize mobile apps to track driving behaviors through the smartphone's built-in sensors. </p><p>4. **Embedded** telematics are integrated directly into the vehicle's systems, providing seamless data transmission and connectivity for insurance providers.</p></p>
