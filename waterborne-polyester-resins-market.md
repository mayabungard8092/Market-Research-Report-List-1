<p><strong>The Waterborne Polyester Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li><li>Not Saturated Waterborne Polyester Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The waterborne polyester resins market is primarily divided into two types: saturated and unsaturated waterborne polyester resins. Saturated waterborne polyester resins are utilized for applications requiring high durability and resistance to chemicals and UV light, making them suitable for coatings and adhesives. Conversely, unsaturated waterborne polyester resins are typically used in composite materials and applications that require cross-linking capabilities, offering enhanced mechanical properties. Both types are environmentally friendly alternatives, promoting reduced volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions in various industries.</p></p>
