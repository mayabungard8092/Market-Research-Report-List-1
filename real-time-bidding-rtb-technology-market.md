<p><p>Real-Time Bidding (RTB) technology markets can be categorized into two main types: open and invited markets. In open markets, ad spaces are made available to any bidder, promoting a competitive environment where multiple advertisers can vie for impressions. This enhances reach and pricing transparency. In contrast, invited markets restrict participation to selected advertisers, allowing for targeted opportunities and potentially higher value bids but limiting competition. Both types aim to optimize ad placements and maximize revenue for publishers.</p></p>
