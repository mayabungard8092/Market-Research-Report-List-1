<p><p>The gastrointestinal endoscopic device market primarily comprises flexible endoscopes and capsule endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are versatile instruments that allow physicians to visualize the GI tract, perform diagnostics, and conduct surgeries through minimally invasive techniques. They are commonly used in procedures like colonoscopy and gastroscopy. Capsule endoscopes, on the other hand, are small, swallowable devices equipped with cameras that provide imagery of the digestive tract, particularly the small intestine, enhancing patient comfort and enabling non-invasive exploration of hard-to-reach areas.</p></p>
