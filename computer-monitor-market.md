<p><strong>The Computer Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CRT</li><li>LCD</li><li>LED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The computer monitor market comprises several types: CRT, LCD, and LED. CRT (Cathode Ray Tube) monitors are bulky and use electron beams, offering good color accuracy but consuming more power. LCD (Liquid Crystal Display) monitors are thinner and energy-efficient, utilizing liquid crystals for clearer images. LED (Light Emitting Diode) monitors are an advanced version of LCDs, providing better brightness and contrast by using LED backlighting, making them more popular for their efficiency and superior picture quality.</p></p>
