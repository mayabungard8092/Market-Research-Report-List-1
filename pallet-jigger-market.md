<p><p>The pallet jigger market comprises two main types: manual pallet trucks and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks are operated by hand, requiring users to push and pull them, offering a cost-effective solution for light to moderate loads. In contrast, electric pallet trucks are powered by batteries, enhancing efficiency and reducing physical strain on operators, making them suitable for heavy loads and frequent use. Both types serve essential roles in warehousing and logistics, facilitating the movement of goods.</p></p>
