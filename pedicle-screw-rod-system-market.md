<p><p>The Pedicle Screw Rod System market is primarily segmented into two types: monoaxial and polyaxial systems. Monoaxial pedicle screws allow for a single-axis movement, providing stability and simple alignment during spinal surgeries. In contrast, polyaxial pedicle screws offer multi-directional adjustments, facilitating better angling and positioning during procedures. Both systems are crucial in spinal fusion surgeries, enhancing fixation strength and aligning spinal segments, ultimately improving patient outcomes and surgical efficiency.</p></p>
