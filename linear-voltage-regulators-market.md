<p><ul><li>Standard</li><li>LDO</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The linear voltage regulator market is primarily divided into standard and low-dropout (LDO) regulators. Standard voltage regulators provide stable output voltage with higher dropout voltages, making them suitable for applications where power supply variations are minimal. In contrast, LDOs allow for a lower differential between input and output voltages, enabling operation in tight voltage conditions, which is ideal for battery-powered devices. Both types cater to diverse applications, from consumer electronics to industrial and automotive systems, ensuring efficient voltage regulation.</p></p>
