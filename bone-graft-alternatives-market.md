<p><strong>The Bone Graft Alternatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Allograft</li><li>Xenograft</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The bone graft alternatives market primarily comprises allograft and xenograft types. Allografts utilize human bone harvested from donors, offering biocompatibility and reduced rejection risk, making them advantageous for various orthopedic procedures. In contrast, xenografts are derived from animal sources, typically bovine or porcine, and serve as scaffolds for bone regeneration. Both options are favored for their availability, effectiveness in promoting healing, and lower complications compared to traditional autografts, thereby driving their demand in surgical applications.</p></p>
