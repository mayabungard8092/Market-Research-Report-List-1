<p><strong>The Controlled Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Controlled Valves</li><li>Electric Controlled Valves</li><li>Hydraulic Controlled Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The controlled valves market encompasses three primary types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic controlled valves. Pneumatic controlled valves utilize compressed air for operation, offering rapid response times and reliability in various industrial applications. Electric controlled valves use electrical signals to control fluid flow, providing precise operation and easy integration with automation systems. Hydraulic controlled valves rely on fluid pressure to regulate flow and are suitable for high-force applications. Each type serves distinct needs, contributing to efficiency in plumbing and industrial systems.</p></p>
