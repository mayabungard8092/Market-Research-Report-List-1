<p><p>The cold stamped aluminum market includes two primary types: single-sided smooth and double-sided smooth. Single-sided smooth aluminum features a polished surface on one side, often used for aesthetic applications where only one side is visible. In contrast, double-sided smooth aluminum has a polished finish on both sides, making it suitable for applications requiring visibility from multiple angles, such as decorative elements and display components. Both types offer benefits like lightweight strength, corrosion resistance, and design versatility.</p></p>
