<p><strong>The Camshaft Bearings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) Bearings</li><li>Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camshaft bearings market is split into two main types: Single Over-Head Cam (SOHC) and Dual Over-Head Cam (DOHC) bearings. SOHC bearings are designed for engines with a single camshaft controlling both intake and exhaust valves, offering simplicity and compactness. Conversely, DOHC bearings accommodate two camshafts, one for intake and one for exhaust valves, allowing for better performance and efficiency. The choice between SOHC and DOHC bearings depends on the engine design and performance requirements of specific applications.</p></p>
