<p><p>The transfusion instrument market encompasses various infusion delivery systems, primarily categorized into volumetric infusion pumps and gravity-delivered infusion pumps. Volumetric infusion pumps provide precise control over fluid delivery rates, enhancing accuracy and safety in transfusions. In contrast, gravity-delivered infusion pumps rely on gravitational forces for flow, making them simpler and cost-effective but less precise. Both systems serve critical roles in administering blood and other fluids, catering to diverse clinical settings and patient needs while prioritizing efficiency and safety.</p></p>
