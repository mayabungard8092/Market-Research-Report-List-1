<p><strong>The IR-cut Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absorbent Type</li><li>Reflective Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IR-cut glass market is divided into two main types: absorbent type and reflective type. Absorbent type IR-cut glass effectively filters infrared radiation by absorbing IR wavelengths while allowing visible light to pass through, making it ideal for applications like cameras and displays. Reflective type IR-cut glass, on the other hand, reflects infrared radiation while transmitting visible light, enhancing energy efficiency in windows and architectural applications. Both types play crucial roles in controlling heat and improving optical performance across various industries.</p></p>
