<p><p>The hydrocarbon market includes aliphatic and aromatic compounds. Aliphatic hydrocarbons are straight or branched chains of carbon and hydrogen atoms, such as alkanes, alkenes, and alkynes. Aromatic hydrocarbons, like benzene and toluene, feature ring structures with alternating double bonds, offering stability and unique reactivity.
