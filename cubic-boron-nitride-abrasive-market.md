<p><p>The Cubic Boron Nitride (CBN) abrasive market primarily consists of two types: CBN Monocrystalline and CBN Micro Mist. CBN Monocrystalline abrasives feature single crystals that provide superior durability and cutting performance, ideal for precision grinding and machining hard materials. In contrast, CBN Micro Mist comprises fine CBN particles dispersed in a liquid, offering a smoother finish and reduced heat generation during machining. Together, these products cater to various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and surface quality in metalworking processes.</p></p>
