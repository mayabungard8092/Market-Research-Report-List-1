<p><p>Milking claws are essential components in dairy farming, facilitating the efficient extraction of milk. The market features two primary types: side-to-side milking claws and front-to-back milking claws. Side-to-side milking claws allow for a horizontal movement, optimizing milk flow from the udder to the collection system. In contrast, front-to-back milking claws feature a vertical arrangement, enhancing access to the udder and potentially reducing the risk of contamination. Both types serve to improve milking efficiency and ensure animal comfort during the process.</p></p>
