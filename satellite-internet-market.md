<p><ul><li>Two-way Satellite-only Communication</li><li>One-way Receive</li><li>One-way Broadcast</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The satellite internet market comprises three main types. Two-way satellite-only communication allows users to send and receive data via satellite, providing full internet capabilities. One-way receive systems involve satellite transmission of data to users, who rely on terrestrial connections for sending information, often used for services like weather updates. One-way broadcast markets focus on one-directional content delivery, such as satellite TV, where data is sent from the satellite to the receiver without feedback, catering to entertainment needs.</p></p>
