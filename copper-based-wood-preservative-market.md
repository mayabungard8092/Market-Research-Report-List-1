<p><p>The copper-based wood preservative market features several key types, including Alkaline Copper Quaternary (ACQ), Micronized Copper Azole (MCA), and Copper Azole (CA). ACQ is a water-based preservative known for its effectiveness against fungi and insects while being less toxic. MCA utilizes finely ground copper and azole compounds for similar protective qualities, offering lower environmental impact. CA combines copper with azole compounds, ensuring robust protection against decay and pests. Together, these options provide versatile and effective solutions for wood preservation.</p></p>
