<p><p>Engineered foams are specialized materials designed for specific applications, categorized into flexible, rigid, and spray types. Flexible foams are soft, resilient, and used in cushioning, seating, and insulation due to their comfort and durability. Rigid foams are dense, strong, and ideal for structural support, thermal insulation, and packaging. Spray foams are applied as liquids that expand into solid forms, offering excellent insulation, sealing, and adhesion properties, commonly used in construction and automotive industries. Each type serves unique purposes based on their physical and functional characteristics.</p></p>
