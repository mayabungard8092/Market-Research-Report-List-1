<p><p>The Automatic Bending Machine market is categorized into semi-automatic and fully-automatic types. Fully-automatic machines, however, operate autonomously, with minimal human input, enabling high-volume production with precision and efficiency. Both types serve different industrial needs, with semi-automatic machines being preferred for flexibility in low to medium production runs, while fully-automatic machines are ideal for high-volume, repetitive bending tasks.</p></p>
