<p><p>Super Swamper tires are specialized off-road tires designed for extreme terrain. They come in two main market types: Bias and Radial. Bias tires have a crisscrossed ply construction, offering robust sidewalls and durability for rugged conditions, ideal for heavy-duty off-roading. Radial tires feature plies running perpendicular to the tread, providing better flexibility, improved fuel efficiency, and smoother on-road performance while maintaining off-road capability. Both types cater to off-road enthusiasts but differ in construction, performance, and suitability for specific terrains and driving needs.</p></p>
