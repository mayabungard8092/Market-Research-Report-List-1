<p><strong>The PVC Medical Exam Gloves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> Powder Type</li><li>No Powder Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVC medical exam gloves are categorized into two main types: powder type and no powder type. Powder type gloves are coated with a powder, usually cornstarch, to facilitate easy donning and removal, making them suitable for quick use. However, they may pose allergy risks for some individuals. No powder type gloves, on the other hand, are designed without any powder, reducing the risk of irritation and allergic reactions, which makes them a preferred choice in sensitive environments like healthcare settings.</p></p>
