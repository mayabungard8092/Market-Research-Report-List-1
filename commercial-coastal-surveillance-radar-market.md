<p><p>The Commercial Coastal Surveillance Radar market primarily comprises two main types: X-Band Radar and S-Band Radar. X-Band Radar operates at higher frequencies, providing high-resolution imagery suitable for detecting small vessels and detailing coastal features. It excels in short-range applications. S-Band Radar, on the other hand, operates at lower frequencies, offering longer-range detection capabilities and better performance in adverse weather conditions, making it ideal for tracking larger vessels over expansive distances. Both types cater to specific surveillance needs in coastal management.</p></p>
