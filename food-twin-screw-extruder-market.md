<p><strong>The Food Twin-screw Extruder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Parallel Co-rotating</li><li>Parallel Counter-rotating</li><li>Conical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The food twin-screw extruder market is categorized into three main types: parallel co-rotating, parallel counter-rotating, and conical extruders. Parallel co-rotating extruders feature two screws that rotate in the same direction, providing efficient mixing and processing of food materials. Parallel counter-rotating extruders have screws that rotate in opposite directions, allowing for better control of material movement. Conical extruders, with a tapered design, offer enhanced heat transfer and pressure control, making them ideal for specialized food applications. Each type serves distinct functional and production needs.</p></p>
