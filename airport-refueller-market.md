<p><p>The airport refueller market is categorized into three main types: self-propelled, towed, and others. Towed refuellers require a towing vehicle for movement, offering flexibility and lower operational costs. The "others" category encompasses specialized refueling solutions like skid-mounted units or hybrid vehicles, tailored to meet specific demands in various airport configurations. Together, these types ensure efficient fuel delivery to aircraft, enhancing operational efficiency on the tarmac.</p></p>
