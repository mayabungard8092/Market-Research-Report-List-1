<p><strong>The Magnesium Casting Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Magnesium die-casting</li><li>Magnesium sand-casting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The magnesium casting market primarily comprises two types: magnesium die-casting and magnesium sand-casting. Magnesium die-casting involves injecting molten magnesium into a mold under high pressure, resulting in precise and intricate shapes, often used in automotive and aerospace applications. Conversely, magnesium sand-casting utilizes granular sand molds to create larger and more complex parts, offering flexibility in design but typically with lower production rates. Both methods leverage magnesium's lightweight properties, contributing to industries focused on reducing weight and improving sustainability.</p></p>
