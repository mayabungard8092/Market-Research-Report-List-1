<p><strong>The Reference Transmitter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>O Band</li><li>C-band</li><li>L-band</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Reference Transmitter market is divided into three key types: O Band, C Band, and L Band. The O Band (1260-1360 nm) is primarily used for optical communication over short distances, ideal for data centers. C Band (1530-1565 nm) is widely utilized in long-haul fiber optic networks, offering a balance of bandwidth and distance. The L Band (1565-1625 nm) expands capacity and enhances performance by enabling dense wavelength division multiplexing, making it suitable for advanced telecommunications applications.</p></p>
