<p><strong>The Professional Studio Headphones Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Back</li><li>Semi-open Back</li><li>Fully-open Back</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The professional studio headphones market is categorized into three main types: closed-back, semi-open back, and fully-open back. Closed-back headphones offer isolation by preventing sound leakage, making them ideal for recording in noisy environments. Semi-open back headphones provide a balance, allowing some sound to escape while still offering isolation, suitable for mixing tasks. Fully-open back headphones create a natural soundstage, ideal for critical listening and mastering, but they leak sound, making them less suited for tracking in studios.</p></p>
