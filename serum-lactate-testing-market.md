<p><p>The Serum Lactate Testing market encompasses various testing types that assess lactate levels in the blood, crucial for evaluating metabolic function and identifying hypoxia. Lactate Dehydrogenase (LDH) tests measure enzyme levels that can indicate tissue damage. LDH isoenzyme tests differentiate specific organ damage. Lactic acid tests directly measure lactate accumulation, often during strenuous activity. Lactose tolerance tests assess the body's ability to digest lactose, indirectly related to lactate production. Other tests may include broader metabolic assessments, further diversifying this market.</p></p>
