<p><p>The dye sublimation inks market consists of two primary types: water-based and oil-based sublimation inks. Water-based dye sublimation inks are favored for their eco-friendliness and ease of use, making them suitable for a variety of textiles and applications. They offer vibrant colors and are safe for indoor use. In contrast, oil-based sublimation inks provide enhanced durability and are often used for industrial applications, offering superior resistance to fading and wear, ideal for outdoor products and high-traffic environments.</p></p>
