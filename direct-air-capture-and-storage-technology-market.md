<p><p>Direct Air Capture (DAC) technology aims to reduce atmospheric CO2 levels using various methods. In the Physical Absorption in Liquid Media category, CO2 is absorbed into liquid solutions, often relying on chemical reactions that bind CO2 for subsequent removal. Conversely, the Adsorption on Solid Media market involves capturing CO2 on solid materials, where the gas adheres to the surface of adsorbent materials. Both approaches are essential in developing efficient and scalable DAC solutions, contributing to climate change mitigation efforts.</p></p>
