<p><p>The E-Field Generators market consists of two primary types: Horizontal Electric Field Generators and Vertical Electric Field Generators. Horizontal Electric Field Generators produce electromagnetic fields parallel to the ground, making them suitable for applications in communications and testing environments. In contrast, Vertical Electric Field Generators create fields perpendicular to the ground, often used in research and industrial applications where precise vertical directionality is required. Both types serve distinct purposes in various sectors, including telecommunications, aerospace, and electronics testing.</p></p>
