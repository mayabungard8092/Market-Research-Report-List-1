<p><p>The oral dissolvable films market primarily consists of two types: oral dispersible films and sublingual films. Oral dispersible films dissolve when placed on the tongue or in the mouth, allowing for easy ingestion without water. These are often used for medications requiring quick absorption. Sublingual films, on the other hand, are designed to dissolve under the tongue, facilitating rapid absorption into the bloodstream. Both types offer convenience and enhanced bioavailability, catering to a growing demand for innovative drug delivery systems.</p></p>
