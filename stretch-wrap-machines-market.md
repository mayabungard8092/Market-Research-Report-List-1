<p><p>Stretch wrap machines are categorized into three main types: manual, semiautomatic, and automatic. Manual machines require operators to wrap products using a hand-operated dispenser, suitable for low-volume applications. Semiautomatic machines streamline the wrapping process, where the operator places the item but the machine handles the wrapping, ideal for moderate production needs. Automatic machines fully automate the wrapping process, efficiently handling high volumes with minimal operator intervention, making them suitable for large-scale operations and enhancing productivity and consistency.</p></p>
