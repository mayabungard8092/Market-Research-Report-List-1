<p><strong>The Surgical Drains Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The surgical drains market is categorized into active and passive drains. Active drains use a suction mechanism to remove fluids, enhancing drainage efficiency and reducing the risk of infection, making them ideal for complex surgeries. In contrast, passive drains rely on gravity or capillary action to facilitate fluid flow, requiring less mechanical intervention. This simplicity makes passive drains suitable for less intricate procedures. Understanding these types aids healthcare professionals in selecting the appropriate drainage system based on surgical requirements.</p></p>
