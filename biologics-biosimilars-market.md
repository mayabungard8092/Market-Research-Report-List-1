<p><p>Biologics are complex medications derived from living organisms, used to treat various diseases, including cancer, autoimmune disorders, and infectious diseases. They include therapeutic proteins, monoclonal antibodies, and vaccines. Biosimilars are highly similar copies of approved biologics, intended to offer similar therapeutic effects at potentially lower costs. </p><p>The Biologics & Biosimilars Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by the increasing prevalence of chronic diseases, advancements in biotechnology, and the rising demand for targeted therapies. The expiration of patents for several blockbuster biologics has paved the way for the entry of biosimilars, fostering market competition and affordability. </p><p>Latest trends indicate a growing focus on personalized medicine, where biologics are tailored to individual patient profiles, enhancing treatment efficacy. Additionally, regulatory frameworks are evolving to streamline the approval process for biosimilars, encouraging innovation and expansion in this sector. As healthcare systems worldwide strive for cost-effective treatment options, the Biologics & Biosimilars Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.5% during the forecast period, highlighting the vital role these therapies will play in the future of medicine.</p></p>
