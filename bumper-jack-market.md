<p><p>The Bumper Jack market includes various types designed for lifting vehicles. **Pneumatic Jacks** use compressed air for effortless lifting, ideal for heavy-duty tasks. **Electric Jacks** are powered by electricity, offering convenience and speed, suitable for modern vehicles. **Mechanical Jacks** rely on manual operation, often using a screw or lever mechanism, providing durability and simplicity. **Hydraulic Jacks** utilize fluid pressure for powerful lifting, commonly used in automotive repair due to their efficiency and strength. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing ease of use, power, and portability.</p></p>
