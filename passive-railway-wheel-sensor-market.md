<p><p>The Passive Railway Wheel Sensor market comprises two main types: Single Wheel Sensors and Double Wheel Sensors. Single Wheel Sensors are designed to monitor individual wheels on rolling stock, providing data on wheel condition and performance. In contrast, Double Wheel Sensors cover two wheels simultaneously, allowing for enhanced data collection and accurate assessment of wheel alignment and wear. Both sensor types play a crucial role in improving railway safety and maintenance efficiency by detecting issues early and ensuring optimal train performance.</p></p>
