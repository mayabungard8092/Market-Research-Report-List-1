<p><ul><li>Jackup Rigs</li><li>Semi-Submersible Rigs</li><li>Drillships</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore drilling rigs industry comprises three primary market types: Jackup Rigs, Semi-Submersible Rigs, and Drillships. Jackup Rigs are mobile platforms with extendable legs, ideal for shallow waters. Semi-Submersible Rigs float partially submerged, offering stability in deeper waters and harsh conditions. Drillships are ship-shaped vessels equipped with advanced drilling systems, designed for ultra-deepwater exploration. Each type caters to specific operational needs, balancing mobility, depth capability, and environmental adaptability, driving the global offshore oil and gas exploration and production sector.</p></p>
