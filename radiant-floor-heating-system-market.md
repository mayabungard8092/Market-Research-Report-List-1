<p><strong>The Radiant Floor Heating System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Installation above Wooden Floor</li><li>Installation Under Subfloor</li><li>Ordinary Concrete Slab or Underground Installation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Radiant floor heating systems can be categorized based on their installation methods. In installations above wooden floors, heating elements are placed directly under floorboards, providing efficient warmth without major renovations. Subfloor installations embed heating conduits within the structural subfloor, optimizing heat distribution and efficiency. Ordinary concrete slab installations utilize a concrete layer containing heating elements, ideal for new constructions. Lastly, underground installations position heating elements below ground, suitable for basements or outdoor applications, ensuring effective warmth in various environments.</p></p>
