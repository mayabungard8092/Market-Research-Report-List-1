<p><ul><li>Gas Injection</li><li>Thermal Injection</li><li>Chemical Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Enhanced Oil Recovery (EOR) involves techniques to extract additional oil from reservoirs beyond conventional methods. The EOR market is primarily categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Gas Injection** involves injecting gases like carbon dioxide or nitrogen to increase reservoir pressure and reduce oil viscosity.</p><p>2. **Thermal Injection** uses steam or hot water to heat the crude oil, making it flow more easily.</p><p>3. **Chemical Injection** employs polymers, surfactants, or alkaline solutions to improve oil mobility and increase recovery rates. Each method enhances oil production efficiency and reservoir performance.</p></p>
