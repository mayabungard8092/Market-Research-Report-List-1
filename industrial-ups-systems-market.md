<p><p>Industrial UPS (Uninterruptible Power Supply) systems are categorized mainly into two types: DC Industrial UPS and AC Industrial UPS. DC Industrial UPS systems typically provide backup power for equipment that operates on direct current, making them ideal for telecommunications and data centers. In contrast, AC Industrial UPS systems are designed to support alternating current applications, commonly used in manufacturing, industrial processes, and critical infrastructure. Both types ensure uninterrupted power supply, protecting sensitive equipment from voltage fluctuations and outages.</p></p>
