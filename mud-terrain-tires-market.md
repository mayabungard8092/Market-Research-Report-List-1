<p><p>Mud terrain tires are designed for off-road performance, featuring aggressive tread patterns for superior traction in muddy, rocky, and uneven terrains. The market includes two primary types: radial tires and bias-ply tires. Radial tires have flexible sidewalls and reinforced belts, offering better fuel efficiency, smoother rides, and longer tread life on mixed surfaces. Bias-ply tires, with crisscrossed layers, provide stiffer sidewalls and enhanced durability for extreme off-road conditions but may sacrifice comfort and on-road performance. Both cater to specific off-road needs.</p></p>
