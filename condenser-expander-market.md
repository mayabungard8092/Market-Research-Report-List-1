<p><p>The condenser expander market includes various types of expanders, primarily the conical expander and flared pipe expander. Conical expanders facilitate gradual expansion of fluids, optimizing flow and reducing pressure drop, making them suitable for heat exchangers. Flared pipe expanders, on the other hand, create an outward flaring at the pipe's end, enhancing connection integrity and accommodating thermal expansion. Both types play crucial roles in improving efficiency and performance in thermal systems, catering to diverse industrial applications.</p></p>
