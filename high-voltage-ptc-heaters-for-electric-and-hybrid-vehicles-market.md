<p><p>High Voltage PTC heaters in electric and hybrid vehicles are categorized into two main types: air-based and water-based heaters. Air-based high voltage PTC heaters utilize heated air for cabin and component temperature regulation, offering quick response and efficiency. In contrast, water-based high voltage PTC heaters circulate heated coolant, providing effective thermal management for both the cabin and the battery system. Both types enhance performance, ensure safety, and contribute to the overall efficiency of electric and hybrid vehicles.</p></p>
