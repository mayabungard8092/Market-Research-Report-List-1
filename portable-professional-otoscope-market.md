<p><p>The Portable Professional Otoscope market is primarily segmented into two types based on light source: Halogen Lamp and LED Lamp. Halogen lamps provide a bright and warm light, often favored for their superior color accuracy and visibility during examination. However, they tend to generate more heat and require more frequent bulb replacements. In contrast, LED lamps are energy-efficient, have a longer lifespan, and produce less heat, making them increasingly popular among healthcare professionals for improved portability and durability in various clinical settings.</p></p>
