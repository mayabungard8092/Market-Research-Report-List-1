<p><ul><li>Un-Cooled Thermal Imaging</li><li>Cryogenically Cooled Thermal Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermal imaging market is primarily divided into two types: un-cooled thermal imaging and cryogenically cooled thermal imaging. Un-cooled thermal imaging uses sensors that operate at ambient temperatures, making them compact, lightweight, and cost-effective, ideal for various applications such as automotive and security. In contrast, cryogenically cooled thermal imaging employs cooling systems to enhance sensor sensitivity, providing high-resolution and accurate thermal data, which is beneficial for advanced military and scientific applications where precision is critical.</p></p>
