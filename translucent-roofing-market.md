<p><p>The translucent roofing market includes various materials like FRP tiles, PC sheets, PET sheets, PMMA sheets, and PP sheets, each offering unique benefits. FRP tiles are lightweight, durable, and resistant to UV rays, ideal for industrial and agricultural applications. PC sheets provide high impact resistance and thermal insulation, suitable for greenhouses and skylights. PET sheets are eco-friendly and recyclable, often used in packaging and roofing. PMMA sheets offer excellent clarity and weather resistance, perfect for decorative and architectural uses. PP sheets are cost-effective, chemical-resistant, and used in industrial roofing and temporary structures.</p></p>
